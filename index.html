services

NHIF huduma center branch offers a number of services.This are the services offered

PAYMENTS

If you come across any issues when trying to pay for your account you can always come to the Huduma branch for help.If you want to pay for your account though Mpesa yoy go to paybill in your Mpesa and choose Business number.The business number is 200222 and account number is your ID number.Make sure you dont Copy paste your ID number otherwise it wont work.

REGISTRATION

You can register for NHIF services at Huduma center.First you have to go to the huduma center website and book an appointment.On the dayof appointment come to huduma center front desk and you will be giuded to the NHIF counter. If you are doing self registration make sure you come with your Original Identification card and Ksh 1,500 in your Mpesa. If you are doing company registration you have to come with an introduction letter from the company for you to be registered.

AMENDMENTS

In NHIF Huduma branch we also offer Amendment services.If you want your child to be added to your NHIF account you need to come with the childs original birth certificate and a photocopy of your Identification card. If you want you wife or husband to be added you come with the original marrige certificate or an original affidavit from a lawyer and both Identification cards.

ERROR HANDLING

If you have any issues with your account you can come to the Huduma branch so that we can solve the problem for you.

Recent Changes

Change 1

Biometrics

Biometrics are not done in the NHIF huduma branch, if you want your biometrics to be taken you have to go to the NHIF offices so that your fingerprints can be taken.

Change 2

CARDS

Since we have moved the new system to biometrics cars are no longer given out to people since they have no use anymore.If you need your NHIF number you will go to any NHIF office and they will give you a paper which your NHIF number will be written on.

Change 3

STATEMENTS

Statements are no longer printe in the huduma center branch due to technical difficulties. If you compltely need you statement printed out you go the NHIF building in upperhill and the statement will be printed out for you.

Change 4

Registrarion Forms

Registration forms are no longer needed when coming to register for NHIF.Whwn coming to register just come with your national Identification card and Ksh 1,500 and you will be registered.

Change 5

Changing of Facilities

when changing your hospital facility just keep note that it will take three months for you to start using the hospital you have changes to.
